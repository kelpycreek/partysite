So....whats a murder mystery anyway?

There are a lot of ways to run a good murder mystery, and some of them are very different from each other. In this one, the actual murder mystery takes a back seat to creating a general on theme party ambiance. When you arrive you'll be given a character with their own story, goals, and relationships. Having a character serves three purposes:

  • It gives you a reason to talk to people at the party you might not otherwise have talked to.
  • It helps create a cool ambiance, so that you really feel like you're in a Dracula/Gatsby style speakeasy
  • Roleplaying is fun! Enjoy trying to talk and act like a person at an exclusive late night party for monsters.

Some of the characters at the murder mystery might care about the whodunnit, but a large chunk of them will be too busy with their own subplots to bother with the titular riddle. After all... that's somebody elses job, right?
